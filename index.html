Yixuan He (何奕萱)

My research interests focus on, but not limited to graph neural networks, machine learning and mathematical modelling. I am currently a PhD student in Department of Statistics at University of Oxford, starting 2020.
It is my pleasure to be supervised by Prof. Gesine Reinert and Prof. Mihai Cucuringu.
In my free time, I enjoy badminton, table tennis, piano, photography and travelling.
Feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research or my hobbies.
