Yixuan He (何奕萱)

I am an incoming Assistant Professor at Arizona State University. My research interests focus on, but not limited to graph neural networks, machine learning and mathematical modelling. In 2024, I completed my PhD (DPhil) degree in the Department of Statistics at University of Oxford (started in 2020).
It is my pleasure to be supervised by Prof. Gesine Reinert and Prof. Mihai Cucuringu.
In my free time, I enjoy table tennis (2022-2024 squad member and 2023-2024 President of Oxford University Table Tennis Club (OUTTC), winning Oxford Blues Award in the 2023/24 academic year), badminton (2022-2024 squad member of Oxford University Badminton Club (OUBaC)), piano, photography and travelling.
Feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research or my hobbies.
